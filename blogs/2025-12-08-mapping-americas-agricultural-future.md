The post examines Farms Under Threat 2040, a national land-use model from the University of Wisconsin's SAGE Lab commissioned by the American Farmland Trust, projecting that millions of acres of U.S. farmland could be lost to development by 2040. It looks at where that loss occurs and how it can be avoided.
