Regrid refreshed over 11.4 million parcels across 259 counties, improving average parcel age to 197 days, and shifted from state-by-state targeting to focus on over 500 high-priority counties representing more than half the U.S. population. The update also improves Feature Service compatibility with Esri's modern Map Viewer ahead of the classic version's spring 2026 retirement.
