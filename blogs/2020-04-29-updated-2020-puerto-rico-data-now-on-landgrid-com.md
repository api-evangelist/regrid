Big Announcement: Updated Puerto Rico data from 2020, now available at Landgrid.com. We are thrilled to announce that we now have the most recent, 2020 data available for Puerto Rico. Well, we’ve had Puerto Rico data for some years -- and helped develop a plan and budget for a community-driven island-wide property survey that could still come to life one day if the time is right -- however, since 2016, we were unable to get updated, refreshed data for the island.
