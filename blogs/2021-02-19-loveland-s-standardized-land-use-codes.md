The parcel data made available by counties across the US varies widely in how land use is classified (if that information is available at all). Our standardized land use codes describe each parcel across five dimensions ( Activity , Function , Structure , Site , and Ownership ). These codes are 4-digit numbers, with the first digit denoting the broadest category, and each successive digit describing the parcel in more detail.
