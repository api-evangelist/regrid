Regrid's February update refreshed 18.9 million parcels across 278 counties, raised average parcel age to 207 days, and added 87,515 new parcels. The Feature Service now supports Related Records in the ESRI AGOL Map Viewer for viewing matched add-ons like building footprints and zoning at the parcel level, and Regrid is soliciting user feedback on schema changes via a survey.
